HandiCraft

HandiCraft is a native IOS and Android mobile application that allows artists and craftsmen to sell their work online with a focus on selling local.


Client: CareerFoundry
Brief: To design the IOS and Android version of a mobile app of my choice that solves a specific user problem.
Role: UI/UX Designer
Duration: 4 weeks
Tools: Adobe XD, Canva, Human Interface guidelines, Material Design guidelines

Overview

HandiCraft is a native IOS and Android application that gives full time artists, craftsmen, and hobbyists an option to reach more potential buyers (local and nationwide) for their unique and original work.

Application Functions


Sign in with Facebook, Google, or email
Ability to browse and purchase without signing in
View and ssearch for local items
View items by categories
Save items for viewing later
Secure payment
Direct messaging
View artists' shop

Problem

Currently on the IOS App Store and Android Play Store there are a plethora of ecommerce options selling new items, recycled, and upcycled items with makes it easy for the user to find the items that a user is searching for. Out of all the options, there is not an ecommerce app focused on local artisanry.

Solution

With HandiCraft, the user gains flexibility to view work created by artists and craftsmen within their local community along with the ability to view handmade art regardless of the users' location.
